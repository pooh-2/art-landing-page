ABOUT ARTIST PROJECT

SHOW INFO

Discover original works of art from over 200 independent artists from across Canada and join collectors and curators, gallerists and designers, who make Artist Project their destination for new talent. Here you can meet and buy art directly from the artists and support the next generation of Canadian artists.

PURPOSE STATEMENT

To create an environment that ignites enriching conversation, for a more personal experience with art.

We are the place that connects art lovers with art makers. Because we believe that how we view art is a reflection of our human experience.

SUSTAINABILITY STATEMENT

Produced by Informa Connect, Artist Project is committed to running a socially and environmentally sustainable event. We do this through partnerships with non-profits and charities, engaging with small businesses in our local economy, working with other companies which put sustainability first, making sure our show is accessible and choosing recyclable or renewable materials wherever possible.

PLACING SAFETY AT THE HEART OF OUR EVENTS

Artist Project and all Informa events will be run according to official government and local authority guidance in the first instance, as well as any venue or location-specific regulations. In addition, we will follow The Ten Informa AllSecure Priority Commitments and when applicable and possible, the fuller range of standards and guidelines described in the Informa AllSecure Guidebook.
