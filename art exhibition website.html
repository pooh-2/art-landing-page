FEATURES

Artist Project is a curated show. All artist applications are reviewed by a selection committee of art professionals and design experts. Every year we invite a new group of jurors so that the fair continues to remain diverse, dynamic and fresh. The jury selects exhibitors based on quality, skill, originality, vision and more.

LIVE DJ SETS

FAISHON

Fashion Art Toronto will turn the market hallways into a runway with an over-the-top fashion show. Models will be dressed in designer pieces available for sale in the new F.A.T Boutique, new to the market this year.
